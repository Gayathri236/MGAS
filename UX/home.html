About Micro Green Garden House

Micro Green Garden House is a leading producer and supplier of premium microgreens in Sri Lanka with more than 10 years of experience. We grow natural and chemical-free microgreens using sustainable farming methods and controlled growing environments. Our products are supplied to luxury hotels, restaurants, cafés, and supermarkets across Sri Lanka while maintaining the highest quality and hygiene standards.
