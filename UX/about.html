About Micro Green Garden House

Premium Microgreens Supplier in Sri Lanka Since 2015

Our Story

Micro Green Garden House, founded by I.G. Gunarathna in 2015, is a trusted producer and supplier of premium microgreens in Sri Lanka. Based in Matale, the company has built a strong reputation for delivering fresh, natural, and chemical-free microgreens to hotels, restaurants, cafés, and supermarkets across the island. Our microgreens are grown in carefully controlled environments using sustainable farming methods that preserve their freshness, vibrant color, rich flavor, and nutritional value. Over the years, we have developed strong partnerships with the hospitality industry while continuously improving our growing techniques and expanding our product range.

Our Mission

To grow and supply the freshest and healthiest microgreens using sustainable and environmentally responsible farming methods while supporting modern culinary innovation and healthy lifestyles.

Our Vision

To become Sri Lanka's leading microgreens supplier and expand into international markets while maintaining exceptional quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Founder

I.G. Gunarathna founded Micro Green Garden House with a vision of providing high-quality, natural microgreens to Sri Lanka's hospitality and culinary industries. With dedication and innovation, the company has grown into a trusted supplier serving luxury hotels and restaurants across the island.
